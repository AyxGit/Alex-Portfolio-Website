About Me

Alex Anderson

Full Stack Developer

       Typical of my generation, I got access to the internet far too early. No 10-year-old should have that kind of power. It wasn’t long before I discovered that you can host flash games on your own website, completely bypassing the school’s firewalls. I quickly became a celebrity among my peers, and as the people around me encouraged my techy antics I adopted and revelled in the role of computer wiz kid. Friends, family members, friends of family members, the works - all came to me for the limitless knowledge that a rudimentary understanding of how to use google.ca bestowed upon me. My 13-year-old self was entrusted with the care of thousands of dollars of electronics. Payment was in chocolate, adoration and good wishes, and to this day I have yet to brick a computer... so maybe they were on to something.


       A few after that I went to Durham College for Computer Programming and Analysis. From here I honed the skills I’d learned over the years, furthering my understanding of object-oriented programming. I started working at the AI-Hub, which was my first interaction with the business world. I learned how to manage client requests and communicate effectively in a team setting. The AI-hub’s environment also enabled me to learn a wide variety of technologies and concepts, namely Angular, Ionic, Django.
